*Last updated: May 2026*

## Data Collection

BodyMetrics is a **Garmin Connect IQ widget** that runs entirely on your Garmin device. It does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers or third parties.

All data is stored **locally on your device** only.

## Data Stored on Device

BodyMetrics stores the following data locally on your Garmin device:

- **Body measurements**: body fat (%), muscle mass (kg), body water (%), bone mass (kg) — entered manually by the user
- **Measurement history**: up to 90 days of recorded values per metric
- **Personal goals**: target values configured by the user in the Target Editor
- **App settings and preferences**

## Data from Garmin Connect

BodyMetrics reads the following data from your Garmin Connect profile via the official Garmin Connect IQ API:

- Body weight (kg) — if synced via Garmin Connect
- Height, sex, and date of birth — from your Garmin profile

This data is read-only and is **never transmitted outside your device** by BodyMetrics.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all data stored by BodyMetrics at any time by using the **Reset All Data** option in the app settings menu. Uninstalling the app from your device also removes all locally stored data.

## Third-Party Services

BodyMetrics does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party SDKs. It does not connect to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

BodyMetrics does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not transmit any data and is safe for general use.

## Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
